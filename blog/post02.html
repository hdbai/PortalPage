Bakery Website Design Process

April 22th, 2017

The most difficult part of bakery website design is how to choose the best theme for your website. The color of text and background image really matter in your design. So I end up keeping my website very clean and simple since I am not a fun of adding too much decoration on the margin and boxes. For the navigation part, each link should go to the part where the infomation is located, so it's more like a jump down button. In the middle of the comp, I put most of the important content there. I choose to have image and text split the web to half, so the image is on the left and text is on the right or vice versa. For the contact us form, I decided not to use any image for it. The reason is I want client focus on the text they try to provide to us and don't get distracted. For the blog part, I start using image to attract people to read our blog. Here's my design:
