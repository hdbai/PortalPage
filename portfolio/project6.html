8-bit CPU

Tool: LOGISIM

Problem:

A simple processor that is 8 bits wide and can do various register transfers and ALU operations over a common bus. The tool we are using is called Logisim.

Solution:

In order to complete this big project, I break down the whole project into several small components, and they are one 4-bit arithmetic-logic unit (ALU), nine 8-bit registers, Decoders, Multiplexers and one 32 x 24 RAM module. ALU will do the most calculations.
